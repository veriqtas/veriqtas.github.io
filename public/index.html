Welcome

Welcome to the VERIqTAS Workshop 2026, to be held at École Normale Supérieure de Lyon from March 23 to 25, 2026.

This workshop aims to bring together researchers working on quantum information, verification, and related fields. We look forward to engaging talks, lively discussions, and exciting collaborations.

Program

A detailed program will be published soon. Below is a provisional structure:

Time Event
Day 1 (Mar 23) Opening, keynote talk, sessions, poster
Day 2 (Mar 24) Invited talks, contributed presentations
Day 3 (Mar 25) Final sessions, panel, closing remarks

The workshop will take place at École Normale Supérieure de Lyon, France.

The venue is accessible via public transport (metro, tram, bus). Further details (map, lodging) to come.
